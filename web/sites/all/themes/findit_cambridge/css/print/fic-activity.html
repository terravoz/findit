Middle School Activities Club

This is an ongoing program.
DHSP Cambridge Youth Programs
Report as outdated or inaccurate

Last updated on July 2, 2018

This City wide club provides fun field trips, workshops and events for Middle School Students to develop socially through a variety of safe, structured, and supervised activities. The Middle School Activities Club brings together youth from all middle schools and youth centers.
